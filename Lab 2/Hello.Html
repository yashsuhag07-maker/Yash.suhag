Amazon Logo

Amazon is one of the world’s largest online marketplaces. It was founded by Jeff Bezos in 1994 as an online bookstore. Today, Amazon sells everything from books to electronics, clothing, and groceries. It also offers services like Amazon Prime, AWS (Amazon Web Services), and Kindle. Amazon is known for fast delivery, wide product range, and customer focus. Its founder, Jeff Bezos, is among the world’s richest people.

pubg logo

PUBG is one of the most popular battle royale games in the world. 100 players drop onto an island and fight to be the last one standing. It is known for its realistic graphics and survival gameplay. Players can play solo, duo, or in squads. The safe zone keeps shrinking, forcing players into intense battles. PUBG has both mobile and PC/console versions, loved globally.

Free fire Logo

Free Fire is a popular battle royale game developed by Garena. It is known for its fast-paced 10-minute survival matches. Players drop on an island and fight to be the last one standing. Free Fire offers unique characters with special abilities. It is especially famous in India, Brazil, and Southeast Asia. The game can run smoothly even on low-end mobile phones. Free Fire also features exciting events, skins, and collaborations.
